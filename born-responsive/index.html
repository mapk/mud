BORN RESPONSIVE

Responsiveness is not an afterthought. It's already built-in to my central core; HTML. Set the spark of rebellion against the ever-increasing Frameworks, and strike back at the lack of innovation.

Check how cool my responsive, inherit HTML properties are. Come to the light side and embrace my natural responsiveness. You must resist the Frameworks.
